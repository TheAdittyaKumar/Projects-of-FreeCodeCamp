BRACU DUBURI: RANKED SECOND AT ROBOSUB 2023

Celebrating the remarkable achievements of BRAC University's Autonomous Underwater Vehicle team.

By Adittya Kumar Chowdhury

August 10, 2023

BRAC University’s team, BRACU Duburi, has made history by securing the second position in the prestigious RoboSub 2023 competition held in San Diego, California. This achievement underscores the team's relentless dedication to advancing autonomous underwater technology.

The BRACU Duburi AUV, now in its 4th iteration, features several critical enhancements that played a pivotal role in this success. The team's innovative approach to design and engineering, which included a shift to a hydrodynamic octagonal hull, significantly improved underwater maneuverability.

Furthermore, the integration of advanced technologies like the Vectornav VN200 INS and a robust lithium-ion battery system has made Duburi more reliable and efficient in handling complex tasks underwater. This year's competition was a true test of the team's skill, creativity, and perseverance.

This victory is a testament to the power of teamwork and innovation.

The competition saw over 34 international teams, but BRACU Duburi stood out with its ability to navigate through challenging tasks such as the "Goa'uld Attack" and "Engaging Chevrons" with precision and agility. The AUV’s modular design and the team's rigorous testing procedures ensured that Duburi was well-prepared for every challenge.

The team’s success at RoboSub 2023 is a significant milestone for BRAC University and serves as an inspiration for future engineers. With continued support and dedication, BRACU Duburi is poised to achieve even greater heights in the coming years.

BRAC University is proud of the accomplishments of the Duburi team and remains committed to supporting innovation and excellence in all its endeavors.
